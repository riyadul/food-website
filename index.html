Kalavuna

Kalavuna is a cooking technique that can use high, medium, or even low heat, which means anything from steaks to burgers to even a whole roast can go on the grill. Cooking on a charcoal grill is trickier

Dal

Dal usually starts by seasoning the beef, then browning it in a hot skillet before transferring it to a covered pot with a small amount of liquid, like stock or broth, plus aromatic ingredients like onions and carrots. An acidic ingredient like tomatoes or wine is usually included as well.

Khuchiri

Khuchiri is a dry-heat cooking technique that uses either high temperature or a combination of high and low. The high temperature is what gives the beef its mouth-watering, crispy, brown exterior, while the low temperature is what cooks it to its proper doneness.

Salad

Salad is like grilling upside-down. It's a high-temperature technique where the beef is cooked just inches away from the heat source. Only, instead of above the fire, as with grilling, the meat is situated below.
